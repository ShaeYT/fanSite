Best Websites To Watch Anime

GOGOANIME

Gogoanime is probably my favorite website to watch anime because they are always quick to upload the new episodes of animes.

9anime

9anime was probably one of the first websites I ever watched anime on. I liked it because of the lay-out and how easy it was to find interesting shows.

Kissanime

Kissanime has a lot of shows to watch and it's site lay-out is a lot more simple-looking.

CrunchyRoll

CrunchyRoll is probably the most mainstream app/website in the world to watch anime, but it has a lot of advertisements during the show that you CANNOT skip.
